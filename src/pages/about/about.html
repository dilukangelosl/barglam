About

GlamBar Facilitates locating makeup artists / Mehendi artists / Stylists and Photographers in the immediate area, thus accommodating your urgent and special cosmetic makeup needs.
We envision to minimize the hassle of locating artists who best fit your rates and needs at an urgent and important time of need, all the while introducing these savvy professional makeup artists and beauticians to the local market.
The app features an all-inclusive online cosmetic store with renowned makeup brands of all varieties.
